
#### Refill-Dry Enchilada Sauce Mix:

- 1/4 cup flour

- 3 tablespoon chili Powder

- 1 teaspoon Cumin

- 1 teaspoon garlic Powder

- 1 teaspoon Oregano

- 2 Pinches of salt

Mix together put in airtight container 6 months


#### To use:

Whisk 1/4 cup mix into 2 tablespoons oil in a sauce pan cook 1-2 mins. Add 2 cups water and Simmer til thickens.
