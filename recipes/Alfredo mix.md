
#### Refill-Alfredo Sauce Dry Mix:

1 cup grated parmesan

1 cup dry milk powder

½ cup arrowroot/Cornstarch

2 table spoon garlic powder

1 table spoon onion powder

½ teaspoon pepper

3 pinches of Salt

Mix together well in airtight container for up to 3 months in Fridge


#### To use:

Whisk together 1 cup cold milk, 1/2 cup of Mix and 2oz cream cheese. Turn heat on medium heat until smooths and thickened stirring constantly,
