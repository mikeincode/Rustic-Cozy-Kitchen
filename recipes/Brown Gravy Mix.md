
Refill Instructions:

- 3 cups all purpose flour

- 3/4 cup beef bouillon Powder

- 2 teaspoons black pepper

- 2 teaspoons garlic Powder

- 2 teaspoons Onion Powder

- 1 teaspoon Paprika


#### Gravy Cooking Instructions:

- 2 tablespoon melted butter or drippings

- 2 tablespoon Gravy Mix

- 1 cup cold water or milk



"Whisk until smooth. Warm over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat. Gravy will-thicken as it cools down."

## 2 TBSP=1 Packet

## 32 Servings

*   *  *Put in airtight jar*  *
