
#### Refill Ingredients:

- 2 cups nutritional yeast 

- 1/3 cups Salt (optional)

- 1/4 cups garlic powder

- 1/4 cups onion powder

- 1/4 cup dried basil

- 1/8 cup dried oregano

- 1/8 cup dried rosemary

- 1/4 cup dried parsley

- 2 tablespoon pepper

Mix everything together


#### To use:

1 tablespoon dry mix to 8oz hot water

1 tbps = 1 bouillon cube
