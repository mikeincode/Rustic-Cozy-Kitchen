
#### Refill-Muffin Pantry Mix:

- 10 Cup AP flour

- 3 3/4 cup sugar

- 3 1/2 tablespoon baking Powder

- 2 1/2 teaspoon salt


#### Cooking Instructions

- 1 cup Milk

- 1 egg beaten

- 1/2 cup butter Melted

Mix well then add 2 3/4 cup Muffin Mix.
Bake 400 for 18-20 minutes



#### Add In Ideas:

- Blueberry - 1cup

- Strawberry - 1 cup chopped 

- Pumpkin - 1 cup Pumpkin Puree, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice

- Apple Cinnamon - 1cup Minced apple, 2 tablespoon Cinnamon

- Banana - 1 cup Mashed nanner, 1/2 cup chopped walnut

- Cranberry - 1 cup cranberry, 1/4 cup sugar, 1/2 cup chopped pecans

- Carrots and Raisin - 3/4 cup shredded carrots, 1/3 cup raisin
