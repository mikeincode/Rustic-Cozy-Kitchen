

#### To make pancake mixture:

- 8 cups all-purpose flour

- 4 tablespoons of baking powder

- 2 teaspoons of salt

- 2 tablespoons of baking soda

- ½ cups of white Sugar

#### To make 12 pancakes:

- 2 cups pancake mix

- 2 eggs

- 2 cups of milk

- 2 tablespoons of melted butter

- 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract


Mix together all the pancake mixture very well in a big bowl the store it in a airtight container

Mix is good up to 3 months.
