
#### Refill-Slap Ya Mama Seasoning:

- 2 tablespoon garlic Powder

- 2 tablespoon onion Powder

- 2 tablespoon Paprika

- 1 teaspoon Pepper

- 1 teaspoon thyme

- 1 teaspoon oregano

- 1 teaspoon salt

- 1 teaspoon red Pepper Flakes

- 1 teaspoon Cayenne pepper


Mix together

Store in airtight jar
