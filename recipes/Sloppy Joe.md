
#### Refill-Sloppy Joe Dry Mix:

- 2 table spoons brown Sugar

- 1 table spoon Minced onion

- 1 table spoon Smoked paprika

- 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

- 1 teaspoon garlic powcler

- 1 teaspoon chili powder

- 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard

- 1/2 teaspoon pepper

- 2 pinches of salt

Mix together very well put in an airtight container up to 6 months


#### To use:

brown 1lb hamburger meat, add
1 12oz Can of tomato and 1/3 cup seasoning mix
