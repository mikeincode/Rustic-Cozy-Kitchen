
#### Light Brown Sugar:

- 1 Cup white sugar

- 1 table spoon Molasses 

Mix together well



#### Dark Brown Sugar:

- 2 table spoons Molasses

- 1 cup white Sugar 

Mix together well


#### Powder Sugar:

- 1 cup white Sugar

- 1 tablespoon corn Starch (optional, helps clumping)

Just blend til it turns to powder 
6 months airtight container


#### Baking Powder:

- 2 table spoon cream of tartar

- 1 tablespoon baking Soda

- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Mix together
Airtight jar
