
- Grade A vanilla beans

- Vodka or Bourbon 80 proof

- Glass bottles


#### To make:

4-6 beans per cup of liquor
Split beans and cut in half. Put beans in glass bottles top with Vodka or Bourbon

Store in dark place minimum 6 weeks.
Shake bottle every week the longer it sits the stronger the Vanilla.

Once it's amber color liquid it's ready to use.
